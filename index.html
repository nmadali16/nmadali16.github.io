Nabil MADALI

I.E.T.R Lab

nabil.madali97@gmail.com

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in signal and image processing supervised by Professor Luce Morin from the I.E.T.R Lab. Before that, I received a Master Degree in Applied Mathematics and Statistics with a major in Data Science from Ecole Polytechnique (X). My research lies at the intersection of signal processing and machine learning. I am particularly interested in the theory of imaging inverse problems and applications in computational imaging. In the past, I have worked on topics including 3D shape classification and retrieval, image and video comparison, image debluring and denoising, and generative models.
